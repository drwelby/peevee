Using Parcel Viewer

Click anywhere within a county boundary to see information for that parcel. Parcel boundaries won't show up until you've zoomed in - they look really messy otherwise - but you can always click for information.

Know the address or APN of the parcel you want? Just start typing it into the search bar, and we'll find it for you.

And that's really all there is to it. Enjoy using parcel viewer!
